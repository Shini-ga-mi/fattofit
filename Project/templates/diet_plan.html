Vegan Diet

This diet focuses on plant-based foods and excludes all animal products.

  • Fruits: apples, bananas, berries
  • Vegetables: broccoli, carrots, spinach
  • Legumes: beans, lentils, peas
  • Whole grains: brown rice, quinoa, whole wheat bread
  • Nuts and seeds: almonds, chia seeds, flax seeds
Keto Diet

This diet focuses on high-fat, low-carbohydrate foods to promote weight loss.

  • Fats: avocado, coconut oil, olive oil
  • Proteins: chicken, fish, eggs
  • Low-carb vegetables: asparagus, bell peppers, cucumbers
  • Cheese and dairy: full-fat cheese, Greek yogurt
  • Nuts and seeds: almonds, chia seeds, flax seeds
Low-Carb Diet

This diet focuses on reducing carbohydrate intake to promote weight loss.

  • Proteins: chicken, fish, eggs
  • Low-carb vegetables: broccoli, cauliflower, spinach
  • Healthy fats: avocado, olive
Intermittent Fasting

This diet involves alternating periods of eating and fasting to promote weight loss.

  • 16:8 Method: eat during an 8-hour window, fast for 16 hours
  • 5:2 Diet: eat normally for 5 days, restrict calories to 500-600 on the other 2 days
  • Alternate Day Fasting: alternate between days of normal eating and days of calorie restriction
Paleo Diet

This diet focuses on whole, unprocessed foods that were likely consumed by early humans.

  • Meat: grass-fed beef, wild game, free-range chicken
  • Fish and seafood: wild-caught salmon, shrimp, lobster
  • Fruits and vegetables: apples, bananas, broccoli, carrots
  • Nuts and seeds: almonds, walnuts, chia seeds, flax seeds
  • Healthy fats: avocado, olive oil, coconut oil
